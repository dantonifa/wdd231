About Us

The High School Salvador Moncada has its name as a tribute to the Honduran Doctor, Sir Salvador Moncada, who made many contributions to science in the fields of inflammation, vascular biology, and cell growth. The mission of Salvador Moncada High School is to prepare students for advancing in their academic careers and to contribute to society in fields where they are talented, whether it be science or the arts.

Address

The High School Salvador Moncada is located in San Pedro Sula, Honduras, in the neighborhood of Barrio Rio de Piedras. The school is near 9th Street and 23rd/24th avenues, in front of Dryclean USA.
