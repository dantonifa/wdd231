Purpose of the site-purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information about the High School Salvador Moncada, located in the city of San Pedro Sula, Honduras. The site will provide information about the academic programs, the teachers, the students, the events, and the news of the school.

Scenarios

  1. A parent wants to know the requirements to enroll his son in the High School Salvador Moncada.

  2. What events will the school be holding this month that promote academic excellence?

  3. Where can I find contact information for the school's board of directors?

  4. What are the benefits of becoming a student of the High School Salvador Moncada?

Color Scheme

  • The color scheme will be based on the colors of the High School Salvador Moncada logo.

  • The header and footer background will be turquoise.

  • The Navigation bar background color will be yellow; and the main content background will be white.

Typography

  • The font will be Lato for the headers and Roboto for the paragraphs.

  • The font size will be 16px for the paragraphs and 24px for the headers.

  • The font color will be black for the paragraphs and white for the headers.
